Governance Model  
The governance model of the SALL Ecosystem Platform is derived from the architecture of the System Of All ecosystem. Governance ensures that technological development remains aligned with the ecosystem’s principles and structural rules.

Governance Structure  
Governance is structured through documented architecture and decision frameworks. The ecosystem follows a layered model consisting of:  
The One Core  
System Of All Ecosystem  
Platform execution layers  
Each layer has distinct responsibilities and authority boundaries.

Architectural Decisions  
Architectural decisions are documented through structured records within ecosystem repositories. These decisions ensure historical continuity and architectural integrity.

Review Processes  
Changes to platform architecture or governance structures must follow documented review processes. This prevents uncontrolled changes and protects ecosystem stability.

Transparency  
Governance processes emphasize transparency and traceability to maintain institutional trust and public accountability.

Alignment with The One Law  
All governance decisions must remain aligned with the principles defined by The One Law and the System Of All ecosystem architecture.
