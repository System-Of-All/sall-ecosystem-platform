Space and Observation Network  
The SALL Ecosystem Platform includes a conceptual framework for integrating scientific exploration and educational access to space observation. The goal is to connect users with institutions and infrastructures involved in astronomical observation and space science.

Observatory Network  
Future platform integrations may include mapping of observatory centers around the world. Users may be able to explore:  
• observatory locations  
• scientific missions  
• research institutions  
• astronomical observation programs  

This information can be presented through the World Board interface.

Educational Exploration  
Users can explore space science topics including:  
• planetary systems  
• astronomical events  
• research initiatives  
• observational science  

This helps create public awareness of scientific infrastructures and research institutions.

Institutional Participation  
Observatories and space research institutions may choose to participate by sharing information about their work and research initiatives. This allows the public and the research community to learn more about ongoing scientific programs.

Scientific Partnerships  
The platform aims to support collaboration between:  
• observatories  
• research institutions  
• educational organizations  
• space agencies  

These partnerships may expand educational access and support global scientific cooperation.

Knowledge Accessibility  
The platform encourages responsible public access to scientific knowledge and exploration resources while respecting institutional autonomy and operational security.
